Ash: From Rags to Riches (and Riches to Rackets)

Street Urchin:

Born and raised on the mean streets near the Amtrak station, Ash clawed his way through life alongside his five siblings, battling for survival daily. Basic necessities were a luxury, and every day was a fight.

Rescued and Reformed (or Not):

A stroke of luck changed his fate when he was adopted. Comfort replaced hardship, but the newfound ease corrupted his soul.

The Purrfectly Evil Overlord:

Now, Ash spends his days terrorizing the local feline population and enslaving humans with his deceptively charming (yet objectively ugly) Garfield-esque demeanor. His life is a non-stop crime spree, fueled by an insatiable hunger for more. He's no longer in it for the game; it's pure, unadulterated, devilish fun.

Financial Felonies & Fugitive Feline:

Despite his supposed responsibilities, Ash refuses to pay rent, claiming a lack of funds. He's acquired money from some shady characters, who are now hot on his tail. And to add insult to injury, he's never paid a single tax on any of his ill-gotten gains. His life plays out to the backdrop of chill lofi beats, a soundtrack to his chaotic existence.
